ROUNDTABLE CONVERSATIONS

Roundtable conversations are small group conversations that all visitors can take part in. The topics will be connected to our main theme, Collaboration. SHIFT 2017 organizes 12 different roundtable conversations. Each session includes one host and 15 participants who share their brilliant ideas, hopes and fears on specific topics. Important! Pre-registration is required in order to participate in the roundtable discussions. The visitors will receive more info about registration and participation in the discussions through e-mail.
