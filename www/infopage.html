About Turku

Turku is the oldest city in Finland and boasts a rich historical and cultural heritage. It is home to the oldest school as well as the oldest theatre in Finland.The SHIFT is continuing this tradition of trailblazing by gathering some of the sharpest minds in the world to actively shape our global future heritage. Located at the south-western coast of Finland it is a gateway to one of the most beautiful archipelagos in the world.

The event venue is the Turku Castle dating back to the 1300th century. At this truly unique venue you can hear the wings of history, while making history as part of the SHIFT.

It is crucial for any individual or company to stay relevant. The SHIFT is for pioneers and thought leaders in the fields of health, learning, and energy. So, if you work in those fields, it is pivotal to join The SHIFT, and learn about the latest trends and developments.

The SHIFT 2017

SHIFT is a business festival where every participant is crucial for making SHIFT happen. At SHIFT you can participate in round-table discussions, one-on-one meetings, and workshops, as well as listen to keynotes and panel discussions. SHIFT will take place at the Castle of Turku on the 31st of May and the 1st of June 2017. Behind SHIFT Business Festival is a non-profit organization Shift Ry, registered in Turku, Finland. Our team of active citizens consists of mainly young professionals, entrepreneurs and students. We are motivated not by money, but by making a positive impact to our society. That is why we SHIFTers, work pro-bono to create a better future. SHIFT is backed by companies and the city of Turku, as well as Turku Chamber of Commerce and The Federation of Finnish Enterprises, while the event itself is put together by local JCI members, start-up actives and students.
