WED 31.5. 19.00-02.00

KAKOLA AFTER PARTY

THIS IS A PARTY YOU CERTAINLY DON’T WANT TO MISS!


Have you ever attended a business party in prison?


Sounds a bit crazy, right? Well, SHIFT will make that happen! Last year’s SHIFT’s Official After Party at Kakola Prison was a huge success. Now it’s back, bigger and bolder! On the 31st of May, the old, notorious Kakola Prison, built for the most hardened criminals in Finland, will be transformed into a relaxing party atmosphere for businessmen and influencers. There will be many surprises waiting for the SHIFT attendants


This year’s party theme is Shifting Realities.


Take a walk through the aisles of the haunted jail cells, enjoy the last hours of the evening in the magical courtyard or take the galaxy stairs to different realities. The possibilities are endless. You can choose to head over to the playful, gaming room, order a drink at the bar or enter the partner lounge area, while you’re meeting new and inspiring people. There will be art to admire, groovy music to dance to, incredible performances to watch and much more to explore.
